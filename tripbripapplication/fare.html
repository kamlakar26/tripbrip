We believe in a transparent tariff structure with our customer. We have aim to provide best service to our customer. Here is a list of fare details for cases you make take in considerations while booking.

  Cancellation:

 1; Cancellation of your booking before 6 hours of journey start has no any charges.

 2; Cancellation prior to 6 hours of journey start Rs.300 fee will be charged. If you don’t pay it for same booking then this outstanding amount will be added to next booking fare on same account.

  Reschedule:

If you want to reschedule the booking while journey no any charges have to pay. Reschedule of booking is applicable as per availability of vehicle.

  Short Journey:

If you complete your journey in a short time or less kilometers distance than standard kilometers per day. You have to pay total minimum booking fare as per applicable for category.

  Smoking and Drinking :

Rs.1000 + additional damaged cost for interior of vehicle.

  Vehicle Damage :

Vehicle responsibility is totally depends on driver. So in such case no charges applicable for customer. If any type of disturb to driver while driving and leads to damage then customer are responsible to pay total damage cost of vehicle.

  Responsibility of payment :

All our rides are billing at the end of journey. So make sure to pay the total fare of booking in cash to driver.

  Tolls/ Parking/ Taxes :

1. Customer responsible to paying all tolls and parking at time of journey.

2. All state entry permit taxes are payable by customer if you travel to other state.

3. All applicable taxes have to pay by customer after end of journey.
