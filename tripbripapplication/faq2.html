16. How many people can travel in allotted vehicle?

According to number of seating capacity occupy. Make sure not to exceed seating capacity of vehicle it will violate the Government's Permit Laws of Transport Department. In that case you will get penalty to over limit the number of passengers. So, make sure to choose category of vehicle as per your requirement of passenger while booking.

17. How do I cancel a booking?

Visit TripBrip website or Application (Android or iPhone) and manage your booking from Profile account section. Just select cancel booking option to cancel your booking.

18. Do I charge to cancel my booking?

You can cancel booking prior to 6 hours before the journey start for no any penalty.

19. What if I want to cancel my booking prior to less than 6 hours of journey start?

You will have to pay Rs.300 as a cancellation charges. Visit tariff page for more information.

20. What if I return at drop location late?

Standard hourly charges will be applicable for- per hour of delay of booking.

21. May I return at any location within city?

No..Booking is applicable for a Round Trip. You must have to drop at same location where you get Pickup. If you drop at any other location, distance from that location to pickup location is payable by customer.

22. What types of payment modes are available?

We are providing CASH payment mode only. At the end of the journey you must pay total fare by cash to the driver.

23. Is fuel cost is included in fare?

YES..Our aim is to help you to enjoy your journey hassle free, so TripBrip pays for the fuel of journey. If you refuel the vehicle you will get refund of that amount which will be deducted from total fare.

24. How can I get refund if I refuel the vehicle?

Initially vehicle will be provided with sufficient fuel for one day trip. In case you refuel it because of any certain condition, then at the end of journey tell driver to submit refuel amount in application or contact us at 022-4942 1122. Your refuel amount will be deducted from total fare. And you have to pay remaining amount to driver.

25. What is the tariff structure? and is there any hidden charges?

We believe in Transparent Tariff Structure and no any types of hidden charges are included in booking. Every booking comes with minimum kilometers fare included and with time limitation. Fixed night charges are applicable for every category vehicle. Visit our tariff structure page for more information.
