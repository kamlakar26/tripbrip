1. What is TripBrip?

TripBrip is a car rental service which helps you to get vehicle on rent as per your choice, convenience of number of people travelling.

2. How can I book a vehicle?

It is super easy !! Just visit our website or Application (Android or iPhone), Signup to create your account. Select the category of vehicles, Fill up the pickup location, time and it’s Done !! Then relax and be ready for amazing trip. 3. Which types of vehicles are available at TripBrip?

TripBrip offers you all types of vehicles as per your convenience. Select category of vehicle based on number of passengers.

4. From which location I will get my vehicle?

TripBrip offers pickup from your location. While booking fill the pickup location and your vehicle will reach at your doorstep.

5. When does the actual reading of Trip starts and ends?

The ride timing and reading of kilometers will start from pickup location. And at the end of journey, by submitting end reading Trip will end.

6. At which time may I start my Trip?

You can start your Trip as per your convenience and time. Just set the pickup time while booking. You can start your journey at any time of day.

7. Is there any restrictions on places where I can go?

NO..You have freedom to travel anywhere as per your choice. Just guide your driver if you want a different travel plan.

8. Can I drive the vehicle?

NO..TripBrip is providing car on rent along with professional driver for your amazing experience of journey and safety. So you are NOT allowed to drive at any circumstances.

9. For which period can I book vehicle?

You can select period of travel as per your choice. You can make booking from one day to maximum 30 days.

10. How early in advance may I book a Trip?

You must book a Trip at least 2 hours in advance of pickup time. We suggest you to book as early as possible.

11. I need vehicle urgently, what should I do?

12. When will I get my booking details regarding vehicle and driver?

Allotted vehicle and driver details are provided 2 hours prior to pickup time. In case of emergency or unavailability of vehicle contact on 022- 4942 1122.

13. How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

We will let you know by e-mail and confirmation SMS as soon as vehicle is alloted. If you have any queries Contact Us at our Customer care number.

14. What to do if driver is late or doesn't show up at pickup point?

In such condition contact our customer care team immediately and we will provide status of vehicle, If alloted driver can't fulfill your booking then TripBrip will attempt to arrange alternate vehicle. Don’t worry if your journey get late because of us we will offer you no extra charges for those period of time.

15. What if I want to extend my journey?

Just contact 022 – 4942 1122, and tell us how many days you want to extend and your booking will extend for more amazing experience. But it will depend upon the vehicle availability which you allotted. So make sure to select appropriate number of days while booking in advance for hassle free experience.
