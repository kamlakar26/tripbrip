26. How much can I travel while my booking?

Each booking comes with its minimum kilometer fare. Additional kilometer journey includes per kilometer fixed charges. For better understanding consider an example, I book a sedan vehicle for a day rent. Means this booking comes under minimum 275 kms journey fare. If I travel less than 275 kms then also I have to pay for 275 kms fare. If I travel more than that then fixed amount of per kilometer is added in total fare. If I make booking for 3 day then booking comes along with minimum 825 kms fare. Visit our tariff page for more information.
